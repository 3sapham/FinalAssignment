Accessibility

Intro

This website, as our final assignment, had to have 5 pages which are a welcome page, a reflection page, an accessibility page, a topical information page and a fun page. The first four of these pages should have the same look and layout. In the last page, the fun page, we could let our creativity loose.

Preparation

Before we started, we agreed to make the general layout and structure of the web pages as simple and clean as possible, but also aesthetically pleasing. We explored all our ideas, and looked at different websites for inspiration, and then decided on our layout and structure based on what was the most aesthetically pleasing and clean. Of course, this changed further down the development road.
